Background

My name is Viktoriia. I am Ukrainian and currently living in Italy.

I like urban legends, Terry Pratchett's works, and coding. This year, at age 25, I figured out what I wanted to do in my life and decided to become a web developer.

My interest in coding started thanks to Tumblr and peaked in high school when we had the subject «The basics of programming.» There I dealt with languages such as Pascal and Delphi, and I did well. I wanted to become a developer in 2014, but due to many factors, I abandoned this decision.

Eventually, it took me eight years to back to my dream. During this time, I experienced one foreign invasion, left one university after three years of study, earned a Bachelor's degree in sociology at another university, changed several jobs, and left my country because of war. I couldn't help thinking I was doing something wrong with my life all this time. So there I am again. I think, my coming back was similar to one famous episode from "Ratatouille".

What are my career goals?

My main career goal is to one day become a full-stack developer.

To achieve my dream, I am going to master the next programming languages: Javascript (including React and Vue), Python, PHP, and Ruby.

Why I chose these languages? Well, there are two reasons. Firstly, I often heard about them from many people. Secondly, I picked up a few free programming courses, tested my abilities using them, and was quite successful. I understand that there are many other programming languages, but I've decided to focus on these right now. I admit the possibility that if my future employer offers something different, I can change my mind.
