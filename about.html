Hello! I'm Viktoriia.

I am an Italy-based Ukrainian, currently working on becoming a web developer.

I am 26, like urban legends and Terry Pratchett's works. I finally understood what I wanted to do in life at age 25.

My interest in coding started thanks to Tumblr and peaked in high school. I wanted to link my life with this sphere in 2014, but due to many factors, I abandoned this decision.

Eventually, it took me eight years to back to my dream. During this time, I left one university after three years of study, earned a Bachelor's degree in sociology at another university, changed several jobs, and left my country because of war.

All this time, I couldn't help thinking I was doing something wrong with my life. Only after experiencing a scene similar to one episode from "Ratatouille" I understood in which direction to move on.

What are my career goals?

My main career goal is to one day become a full-stack developer.

It also includes mastering the following programming languages: Angular, #C, PHP, SQL, and Ruby.

I admit it's a long journey. But after many attempts, I am pretty confident in myself and my abilities.

I understand that there are many other programming languages. If my future employer, as part of the internship or something, offers to learn a different language, I will focus on it first.
