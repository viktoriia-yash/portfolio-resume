Background

My name is Viktoriia. I am Ukrainian and currently living in Italy.

I like urban legends, conspiracy theories (ironically), cooking, and coding. This year, at age 25, I figured out what I wanted to do in my life and decided to become a web developer.

My interest in coding started thanks to Tumblr and peaked in high school when we had the subject «The basics of programming.» There I dealt with languages such as Pascal and Delphi, and I did well. I wanted to become a developer in 2014, but due to many factors, I abandoned this decision.

Eventually, it took me eight years to back to my dream. I experienced one foreign invasion, left one university after three years of study, earned a Bachelor's degree in sociology, changed several jobs, and left my country because of war. I couldn't help thinking I was doing something wrong with my life. So there I am again.

I came a long 10-year way to understanding who I am and what I want to do in my life. Thanks to my supportive family, I don't feel ashamed about it. I know that sounds pretentious, but it's the truth.

What are my career goals?

My main career goal is to one day become a full-stack developer. And I'm working on it.

Front-end development is the first reason why I am interested in coding. I feel genuine children's joy when my code becomes good-looking and works well. I am currently focusing on learning Javascript (jQuery and React) and polishing CSS skills.

Using back-end development, I want to prove to myself that I can overcome everything. For years, I heard that math is not my thing, I don't have to try to change situations, and so on. That's why I am working on upgrading my math skills right now. After that, I am planning to start learning PHP and Ruby.
